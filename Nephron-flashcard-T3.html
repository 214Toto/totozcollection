Renal System

The nephron has 2 parts, name them and their function.
✓ Renal corpuscle - filters blood plasma
✓ Renal tubule - passage for the filtrate that undergoes a series of aborptions and secretions in order to ultimately end up with urine
Differentiate the Cortical Nephrons From the Juxtamedullary Nephrons
Which of the two types of nephrons has Vasa recta
✓ Juxtamedullary nephron
the ascending limb of the loop of Henle of juxtamedullary nephrons consists of the thin ascending limb only.... TRUE or FALSE
✓ FALSE → it has both the thick and thin ascending limb
Briefly describe the Feature or the Components of the glomerular capsule
✓ it has a Visceral layer that has podocytes that wrap projections around single layer of endothelial cells of glomerular capillaries and form inner wall of capsule
✓ A parietal layer that forms its outer capsule
✓ And in between these two laters is the Bowmans Space, where The filtered flud from the glomerular capillaries enters
What increases the surface area for transfer of elements in the proximal convoluted tubule
✓ microvilli with brush border
What is the primary role of the Juxtaglomerular Apparatus
✓ Helps in regulation of Blood Pressure in the Kidney
The major cell types in the Late Distal convoluted tubule and the collecting duct are ?
✓ Principle Cells
✓ Intercalated cells
And what are the roles of the cell types mentioned above?
✓ Principle Cells → receptors for antidiuretic hormone (ADH) and aldosterone enabling them to be involved in Na+ reabsorption, K+ secretion, and water reabsorption
✓ Intercalated cells → have a role in blood pH homeostasis as they are involved in K+ reabsorption and H+ secretion
